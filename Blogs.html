Air India 171: What really happened?(7 July 2025)

It was one fine afternoon in Ahmedabad, Gujarat in 12th June, 2025. The Airport said that AI-171 had landed and was ready to go to Gatwick. A woman (Mrs. Bhumi Chauhan) was late and could not board the plane. Air refueling was done and Air Crew closed the door of the aircraft. The aircraft was going towards runway. 1.38 minutes later, it was high in the air. Suddenly, the plane was going done. Pilot made Mayday call. ATC asked flight number. No answer. The AI-171 crashed into BJ hostel. One person only survived. (Mr. Vishwash Kumar Ramesh).
